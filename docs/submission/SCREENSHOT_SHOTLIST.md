# SCREENSHOT SHOTLIST

**Goal:** Create visually stunning, high-contrast, enigmatic stills that demand clicks on the Awwwards platform.

## Shot 1: The Hero (Cover Image)
*This is the thumbnail that will be used for the main Awwwards gallery. It must look premium, organic, and slightly dangerous.*

* **The Moment:** The peak of the "Curious" state.
* **Composition:** The organism is slightly off-center (Rule of Thirds). The fibers are reaching out toward the cursor (which should be placed near the bottom corner).
* **Lighting:** The organism should be glowing with a very faint bioluminescence. The background must be completely black with only the faintest noise visible.
* **Why it's memorable:** It looks like deep-sea photography of an unknown lifeform. It creates immediate intrigue.

## Shot 2: The Standoff (Close-up)
*This shot proves the physics and rendering quality.*

* **The Moment:** The "Defensive" state. 
* **Composition:** Extreme close-up of the fibers tangling and pulling away. 
* **Lighting:** High tension. The fibers should appear thick, sharp, and tightly wound. 
* **Interaction State:** The user should have just moved the mouse rapidly, causing the organism to flinch.
* **Why it's memorable:** It demonstrates the sheer density of the canvas rendering and the organic tension in the physics engine.

## Shot 3: The Pulse (Wide)
*This shot shows the communication mechanic.*

* **The Moment:** The exact millisecond a Displacement Wave (pulse) is emitted.
* **Composition:** Wide shot. The organism is in the center. A perfectly circular, sharp wave of light is expanding outward, illuminating the hidden fibers in the darkness.
* **Lighting:** The wave provides a stark, bright contrast against the pitch-black void.
* **Why it's memorable:** It visually explains the "sonar/communication" mechanic without using a single word.

## Shot 4: The Absorption (Signature Moment)
*This shot captures the climax of the experience.*

* **The Moment:** The user's cursor is directly inside the core of the organism.
* **Composition:** Centered. The organism is completely enveloping the cursor (which is hidden). 
* **Lighting:** Maximum brightness. A massive bloom effect. The fibers are relaxed, radiating warmth and trust.
* **Why it's memorable:** It captures a rare moment of digital intimacy. It looks like a moment of true connection.
