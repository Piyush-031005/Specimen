# TRAILER STORYBOARD

**Length:** 45 Seconds
**Goal:** Tell a complete story of discovery, tension, communication, and memory. Do not explain the mechanics. Show the emotion.
**Audio:** Record with high-quality system audio capture. The procedural audio is half the experience.

## Act I: The Discovery (0:00 - 0:10)
*What they see:* Pure, noisy darkness.
*What they feel:* Curiosity. Isolation.

* **0:00 - 0:03:** The screen is completely black. Faint granular noise is visible. The audio is a very low, distant sub-bass drone. The cursor is hidden.
* **0:03 - 0:05:** The camera (mouse) does not move. Wait for the Entity's "Impossible Observation" automatic pulse. A faint white ring ripples across the darkness, illuminating invisible fibers for a fraction of a second.
* **0:05 - 0:10:** The user moves the mouse for the first time. The movement creates a faint, heavy gravitational distortion in the darkness. The organism senses the movement. The fibers slowly emerge from the blackness, tangled and defensive.

## Act II: The Standoff (0:10 - 0:25)
*What they see:* The organism reacting to their presence, but keeping its distance.
*What they feel:* Tension. Hesitation.

* **0:10 - 0:15:** Move the mouse quickly across the screen. The organism flinches. The fibers pull away from the cursor. The audio becomes sharp and metallic. Show the organism's defensive radius.
* **0:15 - 0:20:** Stop moving completely. Hold the mouse dead still. Let the "cursorStillness" physics take over. The tension builds. The heavy breathing of the organism becomes synchronized with the audio.
* **0:20 - 0:25:** The organism emits a pulse. The user clicks *exactly* on the rhythm to match the pulse. A bright electric blue wave echoes back. Trust is established. The organism softens.

## Act III: The Encounter (0:25 - 0:38)
*What they see:* The organism opening up. The Signature Moment.
*What they feel:* Wonder. Connection.

* **0:25 - 0:32:** The organism's fibers untangle. They slowly reach out toward the cursor. The lighting shifts from cold white to a warm, bioluminescent glow. The audio shifts from metallic tension to a resonant, harmonious chord.
* **0:32 - 0:38:** The Signature Moment. Move the cursor directly into the center of the organism. The organism absorbs the cursor completely. The cursor disappears. The organism glows intensely for 2 seconds. The audio peaks.

## Act IV: The Memory (0:38 - 0:45)
*What they see:* The aftermath.
*What they feel:* Haunting permanence.

* **0:38 - 0:42:** Pull the mouse away. The organism releases the cursor, but it leaves behind a faint, glowing scar in the space where the interaction happened. The "Environmental Echo."
* **0:42 - 0:45:** Fade to black. The logo "SPECIMEN" fades in silently. The audio slowly decays into the void.

**Director's Note:**
Never show a browser frame. Record in true fullscreen. Ensure the mouse movements are deliberate and cinematic—no frantic shaking unless demonstrating the defense mechanic.
